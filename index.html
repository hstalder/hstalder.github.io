Dylan Places

iOS/iPadOS App

The Songs of Bob Dylan contain hundreds of references to geographical places and historical landmarks that inspired his music and songwriting. This interactive app for iPad and iPhone puts these places on a map and offers a new and refreshing approach to the work of one of the most influential figures in popular music.
Use the link below to get it from the App Store.

App Description

Have you ever wondered where Ashtabula, Brownsville or Delacroix is? Or where «Tangled Up In Blue» was recorded? Or what the story is behind musical landmarks like «Where the Southern crosses the Yellow Dog» or «Twelfth Street & Vine»? Then jump right into this journey from the great north woods to the streets of New York Town and beyond. You never know, maybe you end up visiting that mystical intersection at 56th and Wabasha!

• Navigate through the interactive map and investigate the location and history of both recording venues and lyrical places, search for Dylan places in your area.
• Browse through all official albums, songs and different versions of songs, visualize locations that are related to albums or songs in the map, find out where the songs were recorded.
• Check out the song lyrics on Bob Dylan's homepage and read background information about the locations in Wikipedia.
• Play songs that you own directly in the app (Apple Music integration), make use of the spatial perspective to listen to songs or different versions of the same song.
